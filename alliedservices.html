ALLIED SERVICES
At AASIANA AEROTECH, we go beyond core aviation services to provide a range of allied solutions that ensure seamless, efficient, and compliant operations for airlines, cargo operators, charter services, and private aviation clients. Our expertise in handling regulatory approvals, operational logistics, and specialized aviation needs makes us a trusted partner in the industry.
  • International Flying Permits
    We facilitate swift approvals for international operations, ensuring compliance with global aviation regulations for smooth and uninterrupted flights.
  • AOR Applications & Follow-Ups
    End-to-end assistance in applying for Aircraft Operator Registration (AOR), with continuous follow-ups to ensure timely processing and approval.
  • Foreign Crew Provision
    We provide experienced foreign crew on a contractual basis, fulfilling airline and operator requirements while ensuring regulatory compliance.
  • International Cargo Flight Handling
    Comprehensive ground handling solutions for international cargo flights, including documentation, logistics coordination, and operational support.
  • Priority Access to Wide-Body Cargo Aircraft
    We arrange wide-body cargo aircraft on priority for urgent shipments, ensuring fast and reliable transportation of goods across international routes.
  • Aircraft Leasing & Purchase Services
    Expert assistance in leasing or purchasing aircraft, including market analysis, deal structuring, and regulatory approvals to facilitate smooth transactions.
  • Aircraft Insurance Services
    Tailored insurance solutions to safeguard aircraft assets, providing coverage for commercial, private, and cargo aircraft operations.
  • International Crew Facilitation
    Complete support for visa processing, travel arrangements, accommodations, and operational requirements for international crew members.
  • Aircraft Charter Solutions
    We offer aircraft charter services for various purposes, including:
    • Wedding Charters ‐ Luxury aircraft for weddings and VIP events.
    • Bulk Charters ‐ Group travel solutions for corporate and private clients.
    • Medical Charters ‐ Emergency medical evacuation and air ambulance services.
    • Pilgrimage Charters ‐ Specialized charter flights for religious pilgrimages.
With our extensive industry expertise and global network, AASIANA AEROTECH ensures that our clients receive efficient, cost-effective, and customized solutions for all their aviation needs.
